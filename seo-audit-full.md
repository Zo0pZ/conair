## Executive Summary

Con Air Extract Solutions has the foundations of a legitimate, established business — a clear service offering, real customer testimonials, and a .co.uk domain — but the site is being held back by a cluster of critical technical and content issues that are almost certainly suppressing its visibility in local search. The biggest single problem is an 8-second server response time, which Google will penalise heavily in ranking and which causes users to abandon before the page loads. Beyond performance, the site has no TR19 content whatsoever — this is the highest commercial-intent keyword in this sector and every major competitor ranks for it. The contact page also returns a 404 error, which is costing direct enquiries every day.

**Top 3 priorities:**
1. Fix the server speed (caching plugin or hosting upgrade)
2. Create a TR19 compliance landing page
3. Fix the broken contact page

## Content Gap Analysis

### 1. TR19 Compliance Page
**Why it matters**: TR19 is the dominant high-intent keyword in this sector. Businesses searching for "TR19 cleaning" are ready to buy. Every major competitor (Swiftclean, Ferncroft, EC Cleaners, The Sweeping Company) has a dedicated TR19 page. Con Air has no TR19 content anywhere on the site — not even a mention.  
**Format**: Dedicated landing page + FAQ  
**Priority**: 🔴 High  
**Effort**: Moderate (half a day)

### 2. Location Landing Pages
**Why it matters**: Local searches ("kitchen extract cleaning Bristol", "ductwork cleaning Somerset") are high-intent and relatively low-competition. Competitors like The Sweeping Company and Cleanair UK have location-specific pages that rank locally. Con Air currently has no location pages.  
**Format**: Individual pages for Bristol, Somerset, Weston-super-Mare, Bath  
**Priority**: 🔴 High  
**Effort**: Moderate per page (2–3 hours each)

### 3. Pricing / Cost Guide
**Why it matters**: "Kitchen extract cleaning cost UK" is a popular, low-competition informational-commercial query. Competitors like EC Cleaners rank for this with dedicated pricing pages. Providing transparent pricing builds trust and captures cost-conscious searchers.  
**Format**: Service pricing page or FAQ section  
**Priority**: 🟠 Medium  
**Effort**: Quick win (1–2 hours)

### 4. FAQ Page / Section
**Why it matters**: People Also Ask results dominate SERPs for this sector with questions like "How often should kitchen extraction be cleaned?", "What is a TR19 certificate?", and "Is kitchen extraction cleaning a legal requirement?" None of these are answered on the Con Air site.  
**Format**: FAQ page or FAQ schema on existing pages  
**Priority**: 🟠 Medium  
**Effort**: Quick win (2 hours)

### 5. Blog / Resources Section
**Why it matters**: Informational content drives top-of-funnel traffic and builds domain authority. Topics with clear search demand include: kitchen extract fire safety, frequency of cleaning by kitchen type, what to expect during a clean.  
**Format**: Blog posts (800–1,200 words each)  
**Priority**: 🟡 Low (longer-term investment)  
**Effort**: Substantial (ongoing)

### 6. About / Credentials Page
**Why it matters**: Competitors like Swiftclean prominently feature accreditations (BESA, AXA recommended). Con Air's "About Us" page content is unclear — trust signals (insurance, accreditations, years in operation) are not visible.  
**Format**: Expanded About page with credentials  
**Priority**: 🟠 Medium  
**Effort**: Quick win (1–2 hours)
