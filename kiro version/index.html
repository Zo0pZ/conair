Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about commercial kitchen extraction systems and our services.

How often should commercial kitchen extraction systems be cleaned?

Commercial kitchen extraction systems should be professionally cleaned every 6 to 12 months, depending on usage intensity. High-volume kitchens (restaurants, takeaways) may require quarterly cleaning to maintain compliance with TR19 standards and reduce fire risk. We provide tailored maintenance schedules based on your specific operation.

Do you offer free site surveys?

Yes, we offer completely free, no-obligation site surveys for all commercial kitchen extraction projects. Our qualified engineers will visit your premises, assess your requirements, and provide a detailed quotation within 48 hours. There's absolutely no pressure to proceed.

What areas do you cover in the UK?

We provide nationwide coverage across the United Kingdom. Our team of engineers serve restaurants, hotels, hospitals, schools, care homes, and commercial kitchens of all sizes. No matter where your business is located, we can help.

What is a gas interlock system and do I need one?

A gas interlock system is a safety device that automatically shuts off the gas supply to your kitchen appliances if your extraction system fails or is not operational. Under Gas Safety (Installation and Use) Regulations, any commercial kitchen using gas appliances must have adequate ventilation. A gas interlock system ensures this requirement is met and protects your staff from carbon monoxide build-up.

What certifications and accreditations do you hold?

Conair Extract Solutions is Gas Safe registered, BESA (Building Engineering Services Association) approved, and CHAS accredited. We carry full public liability insurance and all our engineers are DBS checked. We work to DW/172, TR19, and all relevant British Standards.
