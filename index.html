The Business Case

Why Clean Commercial Kitchen Ventilation?

Regular cleaning isn’t just best practice — it’s a legal and insurance requirement.

  • ✓

    Dramatically reduces risk of fire. Grease-coated ductwork is one of the most common causes of serious commercial kitchen fires.

  • ✓

    Complies with legislation and insurance requirements. Your insurer and the fire service may require documented evidence of regular TR19 cleaning.

  • ✓

    Enhances airflow and working environment. Clean ductwork moves air more efficiently, reducing heat and improving conditions for kitchen staff.

  • ✓

    Prevents mechanical failure and costly repairs. Systems kept clean run more efficiently, with fewer breakdowns and a longer service life.

  • ✓

    Improved overall hygiene. Grease and bacteria build-up in ducts can contaminate food preparation areas. Regular cleaning protects your hygiene rating.
